Earlier in the month, we spent time in Greystone Tower, an 18-floor mixed-use development on Idowu Taylor Street in Victoria Island. Here’s a floor by floor breakdown of what the project will offer when it is completed over the next few months:

1st Floor to 5th Floor: Dedicated Parking. 6th Floor: 506m2 (Cafe/Restaurant space) and a gym. 7th -16th floor: 11,190m2 of office space spread across the 10 floors. 18th Floor: Four 3-bedroom apartments as well as a Rooftop/Sky Lounge.

Reach out to Obiajulu Uzoigwe on obiajulu.uzoigwe@cwexcellerate.com at Cushman & Wakefield for inquiries.
